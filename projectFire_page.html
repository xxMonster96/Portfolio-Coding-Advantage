Project Fire

About

Brought about by the way of a D&D Game, Project Fire started as the backstory of a character I made for a D&D session I had one day during my early years in University. Afterwards, when the group dissolved and the sessions grew to be nothing but a memory I still kept on with developing the story. Growing it, creating characters, environments, and lore to fully realize what I saw that day while I sat in the basement of one of my classmates.

Title

Eventually, I signed on for a university class that taught the fundamentals of game design and production using the Unity Engine. I knew I had to develop a story world for this hypothetical game so I chose the story for my character, a man named Tearyn Grimwald, as a basis for the story. Over time, as I spent time within the world of Tearyn I started to see who he was, the characters he knew, and the world he lived in.

Title

I started to think of how I could show this to the world, so I decided to start utilizing the skills I had picked up over the years. I delved into his world and tried my best to depict what he saw through Digital Drawings, Videos, Music, and Character voices. what you see here is a product of those ambitions I had while I spent time in the world of Project Fire.
